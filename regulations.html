R2024 Regulations - B.Tech Information Technology

The R2024 regulations are effective from the academic year 2024-2025 for all B.Tech Information Technology students. These regulations incorporate the latest industry requirements and NEP 2020 guidelines.

Key Features of R2024

  • Multiple Entry and Exit options as per NEP 2020
  • Credit bank system for skill enhancement
  • Increased focus on emerging technologies
  • Industry integrated curriculum
  • Research and innovation focus

Eligibility for Award of Degree

A student shall be declared to be eligible for the award of the B.Tech. degree if he/she has:

  • Successfully completed the prescribed course of study with a minimum of 160 credits
  • Successfully completed the mandatory summer internship of 4-6 weeks
  • Successfully completed the final year project
  • No outstanding dues to the institution
  • Good conduct throughout the course of study

R2021 Regulations - B.Tech Information Technology

The R2021 regulations were effective from the academic year 2021-2022 to 2023-2024 for B.Tech Information Technology students.

Key Features of R2021

  • Choice Based Credit System (CBCS)
  • Industry relevant electives
  • Focus on practical learning
  • Value added courses
  • Minor specialization options

Attendance Requirements

As per R2021 regulations, students must maintain:

  • 75% minimum attendance in each course to be eligible for end semester examinations
  • Students with attendance between 65% and 75% may apply for condonation
  • Students with less than 65% attendance will be detained
  • Medical leave with proper documentation will be considered
