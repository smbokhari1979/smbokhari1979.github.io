About the Department

Excellence in Information Technology Education, Research, and Innovation since 2001

Department Overview

Welcome to IT@EASA CET

The Department of Information Technology at EASA College of Engineering & Technology was established in 2001 with a vision to produce globally competent IT professionals who can meet the challenges of the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Over the past 22+ years, our department has grown into a center of excellence, recognized for its quality education, state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry-academia collaborations.

Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge IT education, inspire innovation, and transform students into socially responsible professionals who can leverage technology to solve real-world problems and serve society ethically.

The department offers a comprehensive B.Tech program in Information Technology that is designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills required in the IT industry. Our curriculum is regularly updated to incorporate emerging technologies and industry trends.

Our Journey

2001

Department Established

Department of Information Technology was established with an intake of 60 students per year.

2006

First Accreditation

Received NBA accreditation for the B.Tech IT program.

2012

Intake Increased

Student intake increased to 120 per year due to growing demand.

New Research Center

College received NAAC A grade accreditation.

2024

Current Status

180 students per year intake with state-of-the-art infrastructure and 11 faculty members.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Campus Facilities

Our department is housed in a modern building with spacious classrooms, seminar halls, and state-of-the-art laboratories. The campus features:

  • 10+ specialized IT laboratories with latest hardware and software
  • High-speed internet connectivity throughout the campus
  • Digital library with access to online journals and e-books
  • Seminar halls with audio-visual facilities
  • Project lab for final year students
  • Research center for faculty and student projects
  • 24/7 computer access for students
