Research Laboratories

State-of-the-art research facilities for cutting-edge innovation and discovery

The Department of Information Technology at EASA College boasts specialized research laboratories equipped with advanced hardware and software resources. These labs provide an ideal environment for faculty and students to conduct innovative research, develop prototypes, and collaborate on interdisciplinary projects.

Artificial Intelligence

Center for AI & Machine Learning

A cutting-edge lab dedicated to AI research including deep learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning. Equipped with high-performance GPUs for training complex models.

Key Equipment
NVIDIA DGX Station GPU Servers High-Speed Storage AI Workstations
Dr. R. Kumar Lab In-charge
View Details
Cyber Security

Advanced Cyber Security Research Lab

A secure environment for cybersecurity research including network security, cryptography, digital forensics, and vulnerability assessment. Features isolated networks for penetration testing.

Key Equipment
Security Workstations Network Simulators Forensic Tools Encryption Devices
Dr. M. Priya Lab In-charge
View Details
Internet of Things

IoT & Embedded Systems Lab

A comprehensive lab for IoT research featuring development boards, sensors, actuators, and communication modules. Supports projects in smart cities, healthcare, agriculture, and industrial automation.

Arduino/Raspberry Pi Sensor Kits LoRa/5G Modules IoT Gateways

Data Science

Big Data Analytics Research Center

A dedicated facility for big data research with high-capacity storage and processing capabilities. Supports research in data mining, predictive analytics, business intelligence, and data visualization.

Hadoop Cluster Spark Servers NoSQL Databases Visualization Tools

Common Research Facilities

All research labs are supported by shared infrastructure and facilities to enhance collaborative research.

High-Performance Computing

Dedicated HPC cluster with 128 cores, 512GB RAM, and parallel processing capabilities for complex simulations.

Cloud Computing Resources

Access to cloud platforms with research credits for AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud for scalable computing needs.

Advanced Networking

10 Gbps dedicated research network with isolated segments for security testing and high-speed data transfer.

Prototyping Equipment

3D printers, PCB fabrication tools, and electronic testing equipment for hardware prototyping and development.
