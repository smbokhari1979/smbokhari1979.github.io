Academic & Industry Collaborations

Strategic partnerships with premier institutions and industry leaders

The Department of Information Technology actively collaborates with leading academic institutions, research organizations, and industry partners worldwide. These collaborations enhance research opportunities, facilitate knowledge exchange, and provide students with industry-relevant skills and exposure.
