Student Achievements

Smart Agricultural Drone System

Final year students developed an AI-powered drone system for precision agriculture that can monitor crop health, detect diseases, and optimize irrigation. The project won 1st prize at the National Innovation Challenge 2025.

Team: Aditya Sharma, Priya Patel, Rohit Kumar, Sneha Nair

Mental Health Assistant Chatbot

A team of third-year students created "MindMate", an AI chatbot that provides mental health support and resources. The project was selected for incubation at the college innovation center.

Team: Anjali Verma, Vikram Singh, Neha Patel

International Coding Competition

Our students secured 3rd position in the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) Asia Regional, competing against 200+ teams from across Asia.

Team: Arjun Mehta, Rohan Sharma, Deepika Nair
